    Born and raised in Los Angeles, California, my interest in design developed when I got my first computer at the age of 12. I enjoyed installing themes, elements, and shortcuts to personalize the experience because not only did it allow me to navigate the interface more easily, but it also gave me a satisfying sense of ownership.

    I’m currently in my final year as a Human Computer Interaction major at the University of California, San Diego. While user interaction and experience design are some of my core strengths, you'll often find me involved in a variety of interdisciplinary activities including software development, big data analysis, mobile rooting/jailbreaking, and even artificial intelligence research.

    I’m interested in working on projects that are not only extensive, but also challenging. My designs and principles are a medium to leave an impression on as many people as possible, from those around me to users who live on the other side of the world. I love what I do.

    Thanks for stopping by.

    — Tony
