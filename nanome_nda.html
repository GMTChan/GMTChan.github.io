This case study is under NDA

Nanome is a startup funded under UCSD's incubator program, the Basement. The product is a collaborative virtual reality platform for designing atomic, molecular, mathematical, and nanoscale visualizations. I worked under the Matryx team, which helped VR users collaborate through the MTX blockchain on the Ethereum network. More information can be found here. Matryx is currently in alpha 2 and can be found here.

I was the first and only product designer in the team at the time and I created the entire end to end experience. I must admit, this work is not as user centric as I would want it to be because of the fast paced environment of a startup. Since I was usually only 1 sprint ahead of dev, I was required to work mostly in high fidelity by my employer.

The final output was 150+ screens handed off to development, not including the many iterations that happened in between.

This work is available on request only (for now).
