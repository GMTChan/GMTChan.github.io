Nanome is a startup funded by UCSD's incubator program, the Basement. It's a collaborative virtual reality app for designing atomic, molecular, mathematical, and nanoscale visualizations. I designed the web platform that helped VR users collaborate through the Ethereum network. More information can be found here. Matryx is currently in alpha 2 and can be found here.

I was the first and only product designer on the team at the time and I created the entire end to end experience. I didn't follow the typical design process due to the fast paced environment of a startup. Since I was usually only 1 sprint ahead of dev, I was required to work mostly in high fidelity by my employer.

The final output was 150+ screens handed off to development, plus the many iterations that happened in between.
