SUN GOD FOR A CAUSE

Every year, ASCE presents a new Sun God For a Cause project to give back to the local San Diego community. This year, ASCE has decided to stay on campus and partner with our friends at the Triton Food Pantry to bring you the first ever Sun God For A Cause Food Drive! Donate non-perishable food items throughout week 2 (April 9 - 13) and help support food security on campus! Check out our Facebook Page for more info on how you can get involved!

DONATIONS

All donated items must be:
• Non-perishable food items
• Sealed or Canned & Unopened
• Not Fresh Produce
• Unexpired (Not Past Expiration Date)

DROP OFF LOCATIONS

• ASCE Booth on Library Walk | April 9 - 13 | 11AM - 3PM
• Food Pantry Booth* - Goodys | April 9 | 11AM - 2PM
• Food Pantry Booth* - Rogers | April 12 | 12PM - 3PM
• Food Pantry Booth* - Rogers | April 13 | 11AM - 2PM

*Thanks to the UCSD Student Foundation, you can make a monetary donation to the Triton Food Pantry! The Student Foundation will be tabling alongside the Triton Food Pantries at their mobile booth. The ASCE Booth will NOT have monetary donations.

Preferable items: Canned Tuna, Canned Fruit, Oatmeal, Cereal, Peanut Butter, Granola Bars

INCENTIVES

Each donation will enter students into a raffle for a FRONT OF THE LINE PASS* for this year's SUN GOD FESTIVAL! YES YOU HEARD RIGHT! Donate at least (1) item and you will automatically be entered to win front of the line passes for you and a friend!

Donate 5+ items and receive a limited edition UCSD Cal Fresh Retro Lunchbox**!

*See the Terms & Conditions for the Sun God Festival Front Of The Line Pass here.
**Limited quantities available per day!
